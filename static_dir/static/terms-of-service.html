About | 关于

dabr.ca is powered by Pleroma, a lightweight microblogging server. Pleroma is compatible with Mastodon and other Fediverse software. A Fediverse site is capable of interacting with any other Fediverse sites. Read more: What is the Fediverse?

dabr.ca 由 Pleroma 驱动。Pleroma 是一款轻量级微博客软件，它与 Mastodon 及其他 Fediverse 软件兼容。Fediverse 中站点间可以自由通讯。中文介绍：《Fediverse 联邦宇宙》。

dabr.ca was set up in November 2022 to escape from Elon Musks's Twitter. It is hosted on AWS and maintained by @wzyboy with configuration files hosted publicly on GitHub.

dabr.ca 于 2022 年 11 月建立，旨在逃离 Elon Musk 管理的 Twitter。本站运行在 AWS 上，由 @wzyboy 维护。本站所有配置文件公开维护于 GitHub。

dabr.ca is closed for public registration, but if you happen to have a Twitter account that is followed by @wzyboy on Twitter, you can send him a DM to request for a registration link. Otherwise, you are welcome to use instances.social to pick a site that is open for registration and join the Fediverse!

本站不对外开放注册，但如果你是一位 Twitter 难民并在 @wzyboy 的 following 列表里的话，你可以发送 DM 获得注册链接。你也可以在 instances.social 上寻找一个自己喜欢的实例入驻，加入 Fediverse 联邦宇宙！
